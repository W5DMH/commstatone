Instructions

    Green: Routine, Good, Available. Yellow: Interruption, Moderate, Caution, Not Ideal. Red: Immediate, Emergency, Hazard, Dangerous .

  1. TO (Recipient): The CommStat program will autofill and direct your StatRep to the Group you are participating in based on the settings in CommStat.
  2. FROM (Sender): The CommStat program will autofill your FCC Call Sign based on the settings in JS8.
  3. Message Priority: Select option which best reflects priority or urgency.
  4. StatRep ID: The StatRep ID will be autofilled by the CommStat program installed on your computer .
  5. My Grid: The CommStat program will autofill your grid based on the ssettings in JS8.
  6. My Status: Enter your general status.
  7. Commercial Power: Select “Green,” “Yellow,” or “Red” to reflect status electrical grid in the location you are reporting.
  8. Public Water: Select “Green,” “Yellow,” or “Red” to describe status of public water utilities.
  9. Medical: Select “Green,” “Yellow,” or “Red” to describe availability of medical support. Docters, hospitals, etc.
  10. Over Air Comms: Select “Green,” “Yellow,” or “Red” to describe availability of public communications such as Cellphone, TV and radio.
  11. Travel Conditions: Select “Green,” “Yellow,” or “Red” to describe ability for road travel and transport of goods at reported location.
  12. Internet: Select “Green,” “Yellow,” or “Red” to describe availability of internet service at reported location.
  13. Fuel: Select “Green,” “Yellow,” or “Red” to describe the availability of fuel (gasoline, deisel, propane, etc) in the reported area..
  14. Food Availability: Select “Green,” “Yellow,” or “Red” to describe the availability of food at the reported location.
  15. Criminal Activity: Select “Green,” “Yellow,” or “Red” to describe the level of criminal activity in the repoprted location.
  16. Civil: Select “Green,” “Yellow,” or “Red” to describe the mindset and actions of the general population at the reported location.
  17. Political: Select “Green,” “Yellow,” or “Red” to describe the mindset and actions of politians at the reported location.
  18. Brief "NECESSARY" clarifying remarks: For brevity this space is typically blank. If necessary to add text please keep it succinct.
